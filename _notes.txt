Localtunnel to share app
1. npm install localtunnel -g
2. start the app ('node buildScripts/srcServer.js')
3. lt -port 3000
    a. alternatively: lt --port 3000 --subdomain cory
4. share generated url

Ngrok requires a bit more setup, but you can secure the app on the other end.
